An introduction to me . . .

Story 

I can't think of a time when I didn't love stories. Ever since I was a child, I've been creating them. I wrote my first short story when I was eleven years old and have been writing ever since. Filmmaking captured my imagination three years later making it my favorite form of storytelling. Currently, I'm working on my debut novel as I study film at Bob Jones University. And although I don't consider myself an expert in story by any stretch of the imagination, I will be sharing with you any lessons I've learned. I believe that everyone--not just storytellers-- can benefit from a knowledge of story.
